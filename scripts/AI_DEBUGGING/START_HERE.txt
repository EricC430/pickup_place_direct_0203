🔧 WHAT WAS FIXED
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Problem 1: "Inplace update to inference tensor" error
   OLD: param.clamp_(min=0.01)                    ❌ Fails on inference tensors
   NEW: param.copy_(torch.clamp(param, ...))      ✅ Safe, non-inplace

Problem 2: NaN/Inf in std parameters
   OLD: No detection or recovery                  ❌ Silent failure
   NEW: Automatic detection + value replacement   ✅ Automatic recovery

Problem 3: RuntimeError during policy.act()
   OLD: std becomes negative, torch.normal fails  ❌ Training crashes
   NEW: 4-layer protection + emergency recovery   ✅ Continuous training
